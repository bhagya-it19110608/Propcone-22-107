Research Scope

Research Problem

Issues which prop masters face when chosing the prop for the cinema industry,
  • Unavailability of sufficient prop gallery and framing into the production company's own collection which limits the casting categories.
  • Hardness of chosing appropriate props which described in the script sceneries.
  • Rich vocabulary is needed for proper result on prop recommendation which makes freshers of the cinema industry feels uneasy for prop finding.
  • Unavailability of open table to find props which has been seen previously on movie or a scenery.
  • Not having modern solution for much realistic prop placement previews than sketching or mini structure creations.
  • Price
Above are the Research Problems we should address.

Research Objective

Make the ease of works of the prop masters with minimum cost, time and labour. Develop and implement a virtual prop house including the renting platform and specified features to make the recommendation in convinient way and modern realistic solution for prop previewings.
  • Scene Reader by images - Reading scene background and prop suggestion
  • Script Reader for prop suggestions - Suggesting props by reading the uploaded scripts
  • Prop recommender by Video object detector - Recommending props by video object detecting
  • AR Background scene designer - Realtime prop positioning for best placement

Methodology

The "Prop Cone" platform of virtual prop house consists of 4 components based on the services.
  • Scene ready by images for prop recommendation
  • AR background scene designer for prop placement
  • Script reader for prop suggestions
  • A recommendation engine based on video clips
